Fishing Lessons: How to Cast

Fishing has many aspects. One of the most important parts is casting. Casting is how you get your line to the fish and it is quite easy to master. These simple steps will teach you how to do a basic cast.
  1. Flip open the bail on your reel and hold back the line with your index finger.
  2. Bring the rod back to a 45 degree angle behind your shoulder.
  3. Quickly bring the rod forward and let go of the line when it is directly above you.
  4. You just succesfully casted!!!!
